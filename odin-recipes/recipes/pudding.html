Pudding

Back to Recipes

Description

Pudding is a creamy dessert that can be made in various flavors, such as chocolate, vanilla, or butterscotch.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, mix sugar, cornstarch, and salt.
  2. Add milk gradually while stirring to avoid lumps.
  3. Cook over medium heat until the mixture thickens and bubbles.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract and butter.
  5. Pour into serving dishes and refrigerate until set.
