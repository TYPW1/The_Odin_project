Lasagna

Back to Recipes

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and onion until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
